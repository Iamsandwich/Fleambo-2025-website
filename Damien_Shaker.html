A Damien shaker charm!
Print and cut the parts, and then laminate or tape the very last part that has hearts on it! All the parts are numbered. In the end you'll have a nice big shaker with a clock face background and a hanging bunny charm! Laminate the pieces for extra strength, add glitter or beads if you wish. Use thicker card for middle layer to create more space inside the shaker (if you want)
