A Blauer shaker charm!
Print and cut the parts, and then laminate or tape the very last part of the jug! All the parts are numbered. In the end you'll have a nice big shaker that look like a jug of juice! Laminate the pieces for extra strength, add glitter or beads if you wish. Use thicker card for middle layer to create more space inside the shaker (if you want)
