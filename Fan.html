A colorful Avel and Blauer fan
The petals of the fan have to be cut out, punched through at the bottom where the hole is indicated and collected together in any order you like!
You can tape over or laminate both sides for extra toughness!
To make sure the fan holds and folds well, add a pin or a tack into the holes and secure on the other end with a bit of eraser, foam, glue etc. You can also use special "split pins" like these for securing.
Also you can add a little ribbon or tassel for extra decor!
resize according to your sizing needs!
